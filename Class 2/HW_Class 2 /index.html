About Me

I'm a Washington, D.C.-based (by way of NYC and Minnesota) travel editor, writer, and photographer.

I currently am Senior Editor at MapQuest and AOL Travel. I have also worked as a guidebook editor at Fodor's Travel, a freelance writer and editor, and a managing editor at a start-up travel website, Travora.com.

My photography has appeared in ISLANDS Magazine, Travel + Leisure's website and newsletters, Fodor's, AOL Travel, Gadling, 10best.com, and Hawaiimagazine.com.
